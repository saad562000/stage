Refund Policy

Returns If you are not completely happy with a purchase made online you can return it to us within 14 DAYS of receipt and we will gladly refund you. Please don’t put this small business in an awkward position by asking for a refund outside of this period. Items must be returned unused, in their original condition, and be sent back in high-quality and secure packaging We can also exchange any item within the same time frame Do notify us by e-mail that you will be returning your rug Shipping to you is free in the UK and Europe and we won’t reclaim this if you return your item. However, we cannot refund shipping costs where you have asked us to use an ‘out of the ordinary’ shipping service such as an individual delivery van for a very large rug or express or named day/hour shipping service We cannot refund USA and rest of the world shipping costs Return shipping and packing should be arranged at your own cost and convenience, using a reliable tracked courier service. All items are inspected on return. Sorry, but we cannot offer refunds or exchanges on custom made or custom repaired /altered rugs Our lovely rugs are vintage and will have signs of wear or use: please carefully examine the description of the condition and colour before purchasing so that you're content when it arrives.
